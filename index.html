Student Life

Dormitories

Superhero dorms are specialized living quarters designed to accommodate students with unique powers and abilities. Unlike traditional dormitories, superhero dorms are equipped with advanced features and customizations to ensure safety, comfort, and the growth of each hero-in-training.

Our Campus

A superhero campus is a specially designed environment where young heroes-in-training live, learn, and develop their skills. Unlike a traditional academic setting, a superhero campus is built to support students with extraordinary abilities, while providing a secure and controlled atmosphere for both education and personal growth.

Hangout Club

The Hangout Club is a chill zone where heroes come together to relax and take a break from their demanding duties. It's a space filled with cozy lounges, gaming stations, and opportunities for friendly competitions—be it virtual battles, trivia nights, or board games that spark their strategic thinking. Beyond gaming, this club also hosts creative activities, like drawing, music sessions, and storytelling, where heroes share tales of their adventures or even plan new missions together.
